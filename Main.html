Facilities

Crossfit

Our crossfit section is designed for serious lifters who want to push their limits and build strength. We have a wide range of equipment, including squat racks, deadlift platforms, and bench presses, as well as a variety of weights and bars. Whether you're a competitive powerlifter or just looking to build muscle and improve your strength, our crossfit section has everything you need to reach your goals.

Cardio Section

Our cardio section is perfect for anyone looking to get in shape, lose weight, or improve their cardiovascular health. We have a variety of machines, including treadmills, ellipticals, and stationary bikes, as well as free weights and resistance bands. Whether you prefer steady-state cardio or high-intensity interval training, our cardio section has everything you need to achieve your fitness goals.

Steam Bath

Our steam bath is the perfect way to relax and recover after a tough workout. The heat and steam can help soothe sore muscles, improve circulation, and reduce stress. Our steam bath is spacious and comfortable, with plenty of room to stretch out and unwind. Whether you're looking to recover from a workout or simply enjoy some relaxation time, our steam bath is the perfect place to do it.
